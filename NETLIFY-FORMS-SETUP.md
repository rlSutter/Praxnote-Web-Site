# Netlify Forms — Email notification

The contact form on this site uses **Netlify Forms**. Submissions are stored in your Netlify dashboard. To have each submission **emailed to you**:

1. Deploy the site to Netlify (drag the folder to [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop) or connect a Git repo).
2. In the Netlify dashboard: **Site** → **Forms**.
3. After the first form submission (or once the form is detected), click your form name (**early-access**).
4. Go to **Form notifications** (or **Settings and usage** → **Form notifications**).
5. Click **Add notification** → **Email notification**.
6. Set the email to: **rebeccasutter@practicaltechleader.com**.
7. Save.

New submissions will then be emailed to that address and still appear under **Forms** in the dashboard.
